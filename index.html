About Courses

This is a 6-week transformation designed for the curious, the builders, the believers, the ones tired of sitting on the sidelines. Below is the course outline, the exact journey you’ll be going through week by week.

Week 1
REBIRTH

This isn’t just about learning crypto; it’s about rebuilding your identity in a digital world where ownership, purpose, and opportunity are back in your hands.

  • What Web3 is
  • How to craft a powerful online presence
  • How to show up daily and build trust
  • The Web3 mindset: From consumer to creator
Week 2
FOUNDATION

Before you can earn, you need to understand the terrain. This week gives you the basics of wallets, chains, DeFi and how to stay safe in Web3.

  • How to set up and protect your wallet
  • What blockchains actually do (ETH, TON, Solana)
  • How to stay safe and avoid common scams
  • The basics of DeFi, NFTs, DAOs, and dApps
Week 3
OPPORTUNITY

Free tokens, early access, and passive income all start with one thing: showing up early. This week unlocks airdrop farming and real research.

  • What airdrops are and how they reward users
  • How to complete tasks and qualify for drops
  • The tools to track and manage multiple wallets
  • How to research new projects with clarity
Week 4
VISION

Price action isn’t magic. It’s a language and when you learn to read it, you gain an edge most people don’t even know exists.

  • How to use TradingView
  • Basic chart patterns and support/resistance
  • Key indicators: RSI, Moving Average, MACD
  • How to spot opportunities and avoid traps
Week 5
DISCIPLINE

This is where beginners separate from the real players. Learn to trade with rules, manage risk, and turn your skill into income or a job.

  • How to follow trading signals safely
  • How to size your positions and manage risk
  • Where to find Web3 jobs, bounties, and gigs
  • How to pitch yourself and land roles
Week 6
ASCENSION

Now that you’ve built your skills and identity, it’s time to step into leadership. Build your brand, grow your audience, and start earning for real.

  • How to grow on X, Discord, and Telegram
  • Content strategies that build trust and influence
  • Income streams: consulting, referrals, alpha drops
  • How to publish your Web3 story and join Phoenix Pro
